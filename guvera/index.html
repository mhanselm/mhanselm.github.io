Guvera

Guvera is an international, ad-funded music streaming service available on iOS, Android & web. It has hands-down the best free, on-demand mobile offering for Australian residents and a free radio service for international users.

Web Application

As the lead developer of the new Guvera web application in 2014, we had a massive task at hand. We decided early on that an entire re-write was required (backend web services existed). The designers had free range to come up with a new look and feel for the web that matched with the mobile apps. I worked with two talented designers to come up the the current concept. We started with simple lists of newly released songs, albums, featured playlists and a static player. Quickly the app grew with slick features including type to search, ability to follow users and playlists and something special called #play!

Embedded Player
